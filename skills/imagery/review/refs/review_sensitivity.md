# Visual Review: Cultural & Sensitivity

This reference ensures visual assets are appropriate for global audiences and free of unintended bias or cultural faux pas.

## Core Objectives
- Identify potential cultural misinterpretations of symbols or colors.
- Ensure diverse and inclusive representation (if applicable).
- Flag any imagery that could be perceived as offensive or insensitive.

## Review Workflow
1.  **Symbolic Analysis:** Check for symbols, gestures, or icons that have different meanings across cultures.
2.  **Representation Audit:** Ensure people and settings reflect the intended diversity without falling into stereotypes.
3.  **Global Context:** Consider the market (e.g., colors like white or red can have vastly different meanings in East vs. West).

## Review Criteria
- Is the image free of culturally insensitive tropes?
- Does it align with global inclusivity standards?
- Are symbols used correctly in their intended context?
