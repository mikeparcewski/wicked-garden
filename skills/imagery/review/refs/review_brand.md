# Visual Review: Brand Compliance

This reference is for ensuring a visual asset aligns with specific corporate or project brand guidelines.

## Core Objectives
- Verify logo placement and integrity.
- Ensure strict adherence to the brand color palette.
- Validate typography and font usage (if applicable).
- Confirm the "Brand Voice" or "Tone" (e.g., Professional, Playful, Innovative).

## Review Workflow
1.  **Palette Check:** Compare extracted hex codes against the official style guide.
2.  **Logo Integrity:** Ensure the logo isn't distorted, recolored incorrectly, or placed too close to other elements (clearance rules).
3.  **Aesthetic Alignment:** Does the image "feel" like the brand? (e.g., a luxury brand should not have "gritty" lighting unless specified).

## Review Criteria
- Are the primary brand colors dominant?
- Does the visual style match the established brand archetype?
- Is the imagery appropriate for the target audience?
