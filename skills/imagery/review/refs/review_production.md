# Visual Review: Production Readiness

This reference is for the final quality gate before a visual asset is delivered for business use.

## Core Objectives
- Detect technical artifacts (blur, pixelation, AI distortions).
- Verify file specifications (resolution, aspect ratio, format).
- Ensure "cleanliness" (no unintended text, watermarks, or background clutter).

## Review Workflow
1.  **Artifact Inspection:** Zoom in on edges and complex textures to check for AI-generated "hallucinations" or blurring.
2.  **Spec Validation:** Confirm the image matches the requested aspect ratio (e.g., 16:9 for presentations) and resolution.
3.  **Background Cleanup:** Identify and remove any distracting elements that don't serve the primary subject.

## Review Criteria
- Is the image sharp and free of compression artifacts?
- Are the dimensions exactly as requested?
- Is it "Social Media Ready" or "Print Ready" based on the task?
