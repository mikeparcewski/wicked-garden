# Strategic Analysis Templates: Competitive & Market Analysis

Competitive analysis, market analysis, and quick decision templates for business strategy.

## Competitive Analysis Template

```markdown
## Competitive Analysis: {Market/Category}

### Competitive Position: LEADER | CHALLENGER | FOLLOWER | NICHE
**Strategic Stance**: {Head-to-head | Differentiation | Niche | Blue Ocean}
**Analysis Date**: {date}

### Competitive Landscape

**Market Characteristics**:
- Market Size: ${size}
- Growth Rate: {%}
- Maturity Stage: {Emerging | Growing | Mature | Declining}

**Competitive Set**:
- Direct Competitors: {count} ({names})
- Indirect Competitors: {count} ({names})
- Substitutes: {alternatives}

### Five Forces Assessment

| Force | Intensity | Impact on Profitability | Trend |
|-------|-----------|------------------------|-------|
| Competitive Rivalry | H/M/L | {how it affects us} | ↑/→/↓ |
| Supplier Power | H/M/L | {how it affects us} | ↑/→/↓ |
| Buyer Power | H/M/L | {how it affects us} | ↑/→/↓ |
| Threat of Substitutes | H/M/L | {how it affects us} | ↑/→/↓ |
| Threat of New Entrants | H/M/L | {how it affects us} | ↑/→/↓ |

**Overall Market Attractiveness**: {ATTRACTIVE | NEUTRAL | UNATTRACTIVE}

**Key Insights**:
- {insight from five forces}

### Competitive Matrix

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Position | Price | Share | Trend |
|------------|-----------|------------|----------|-------|-------|-------|
| **Us** | {strengths} | {weaknesses} | {position} | ${price} | {%} | ↑/→/↓ |
| Competitor A | {strengths} | {weaknesses} | {position} | ${price} | {%} | ↑/→/↓ |
| Competitor B | {strengths} | {weaknesses} | {position} | ${price} | {%} | ↑/→/↓ |

### Feature Comparison

| Feature | Category | Us | Comp A | Comp B | Importance |
|---------|----------|-----|--------|--------|------------|
| {feature} | {cat} | ✓/✗/◐ | ✓/✗/◐ | ✓/✗/◐ | H/M/L |

Legend: ✓ = Full, ◐ = Partial, ✗ = None

### SWOT Analysis

**STRENGTHS** (Internal, Positive):
- {internal advantage we have}
- {capability or resource strength}

**WEAKNESSES** (Internal, Negative):
- {internal limitation}
- {gap vs competitors}

**OPPORTUNITIES** (External, Positive):
- {market trend favoring us}
- {unmet customer need}

**THREATS** (External, Negative):
- {competitive threat}
- {market shift risk}

### Positioning Map

```
        {Axis 1 High (e.g., Performance)}
                    |
                    | [Comp A]
                    |
{Axis 2 Low} -------+------- {Axis 2 High}
     (e.g., Price)  |     (e.g., Price)
                    | [Us]
                    | [Comp B]
        {Axis 1 Low (e.g., Performance)}
```

**White Space**: {Underserved segments or positioning gaps}

### Win/Loss Analysis

**We Win When**:
- {criteria that tilts decisions toward us}
- {objection we overcome well}

**We Lose When**:
- {criteria that tilts decisions away}
- {objection we can't overcome}

**Win Rate**: {%} ({sample size})

### Strategic Recommendations

**Immediate Actions** (0-3 months):
1. {action} - {why} - {owner}
2. {action} - {why} - {owner}

**Medium-term Moves** (3-12 months):
1. {initiative} - {rationale}
2. {initiative} - {rationale}

**Positioning Focus**:
- **Where to compete**: {market segment or category}
- **How to differentiate**: {key differentiator}
- **What to emphasize**: {messaging focus}

**Competitive Risks & Mitigation**:
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| {threat} | H/M/L | H/M/L | {strategy} |

**Opportunities to Exploit**:
| Opportunity | Window | Action Required |
|-------------|--------|-----------------|
| {gap or weakness} | {timeframe} | {how to capitalize} |
```

## Market Analysis Template

```markdown

### Market Attractiveness: {X}/25
**Assessment**: HIGHLY ATTRACTIVE | ATTRACTIVE | NEUTRAL | UNATTRACTIVE
**Analysis Date**: {date}

**TAM** (Total Addressable Market): ${amount}
- Definition: {what market we're measuring}
- Method: {Top-down | Bottom-up}
- Calculation: {formula used}

**SAM** (Serviceable Addressable Market): ${amount}
- Definition: {portion we can serve}
- Constraints: {geographic, capability, etc.}
- Percentage of TAM: {%}

**SOM** (Serviceable Obtainable Market): ${amount}
- Definition: {realistic 1-3yr target}
- Assumptions: {market share, conversion rate}
- Percentage of SAM: {%}

**Sizing Confidence**: {HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW}
- Sources: {data sources used}
- Assumptions: {key assumptions}

| Segment | Size ($) | Growth | Fit | Access | Priority |
|---------|----------|--------|-----|--------|----------|
| {segment} | ${size} | {%/yr} | H/M/L | H/M/L | P{n} |

**Primary Target Segment**: {segment name}
- **Why**: {rationale for priority}
- **Characteristics**: {key attributes}
- **Needs**: {primary needs}
- **Approach**: {go-to-market strategy}
```

```
### Market Trends

**Key Trends**:
```

## Quick Decision Template

For fast strategic decisions:

```markdown
## Quick Strategic Check: {Decision}

**Decision**: {What are we deciding?}
**Timeframe**: {When do we need to decide?}

### 3-Bullet Assessment

**Business Case**:
- ROI: {quick estimate} | Payback: {timeframe}

**Strategic Fit**:
- {How this aligns with strategy}

**Risk Level**: {LOW | MEDIUM | HIGH}
- {Primary risk}

### Recommendation: APPROVE | CONDITIONAL | REJECT

**Why**: {One sentence rationale}

**Conditions** (if any):
- {Condition to meet}

**Next Step**: {Immediate action}
```
