# Strategic Analysis Templates: ROI & Value Proposition

ROI analysis and value proposition templates for business strategy.

## ROI Analysis Template

```markdown
## ROI Analysis: {Project Name}

### Decision: APPROVE | CONDITIONAL | REJECT
**Confidence**: HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW
**Analysis Date**: {date}

### Executive Summary
{2-3 sentences: what, why, recommendation}

### Financial Analysis

**Investment**:
- Development Cost: ${amount} ({hours} hours × ${rate})
- Infrastructure Setup: ${amount}
- Ongoing Annual Cost: ${amount}
- **Total 3-Year Investment**: ${total}

**Benefits** (Annual):
- Revenue Impact: ${amount}
  - {specific revenue source}
- Cost Savings: ${amount}
  - {specific cost reduction}
- Risk Mitigation Value: ${amount}
  - {quantified risk reduction}
- **Total Annual Benefit**: ${amount}

**Returns**:
- **ROI**: {percentage}%
- **Payback Period**: {X} years {Y} months
- **3-Year NPV** (@10%): ${amount}
- **Break-even**: {Yes/No}

### Strategic Alignment

**Business Objectives**:
- {How this supports strategic goal 1}
- {How this supports strategic goal 2}

**Strategic Fit**: {HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW}

**Prioritization**:
- Ranking vs other investments: {position}
- Resource availability: {assessment}
- Market timing: {assessment}

### Assumptions & Confidence

**Key Assumptions**:
- {assumption 1} - {confidence level}
- {assumption 2} - {confidence level}

**Data Sources**:
- {source for cost estimates}
- {source for benefit projections}

**Confidence Factors**:
- {What increases confidence}
- {What decreases confidence}

### Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy | Owner |
|------|-------------|--------|---------------------|-------|
| {risk} | H/M/L | H/M/L | {strategy} | {who} |

### Recommendation

**Primary Recommendation**: {APPROVE | CONDITIONAL | REJECT}

**Rationale**:
{Why this recommendation}

**Conditions** (if CONDITIONAL):
1. {condition to be met}
2. {condition to be met}

**Action Items**:
1. {action} - {owner} - {deadline}
2. {action} - {owner} - {deadline}

**Next Steps**:
- {immediate next step}
- {follow-up action}

### Appendix

**Calculation Details**:
{Detailed ROI calculations}

**Alternative Scenarios**:
- Best case: ROI {%}, Payback {months}
- Base case: ROI {%}, Payback {months}
- Worst case: ROI {%}, Payback {months}
```

## Value Proposition Template

```markdown
## Value Proposition: {Product/Feature}

### Value Score: {X}/25
**Strength**: STRONG | SOLID | WEAK | NOT VIABLE

### Value Statement

**For** {target customer segment}
**Who** {statement of need or opportunity}
**Our** {product/service name}
**Is a** {product category}
**That** {key benefit/value}
**Unlike** {primary alternative or competitor}
**We** {primary differentiation}

### Customer Profile

**Target Customer**: {description}
- {demographic/firmographic details}
- {behavioral characteristics}
- {key attributes}

**Jobs-to-be-Done**:

*Functional Jobs* (tasks):
- {task they're trying to complete}
- {task they're trying to complete}

*Emotional Jobs* (feelings):
- {how they want to feel}
- {frustration to avoid}

*Social Jobs* (perception):
- {how they want to be perceived}
- {status they seek}

### Pain & Gain Analysis

**Current Pains**:
| Pain Point | Severity | Frequency | Our Solution | Impact |
|------------|----------|-----------|--------------|--------|
| {pain} | H/M/L | H/M/L | {how we solve} | H/M/L |

**Desired Gains**:
| Desired Outcome | Importance | How We Deliver | Impact |
|-----------------|------------|----------------|--------|
| {gain} | H/M/L | {our approach} | H/M/L |

### Differentiation Analysis

**Primary Differentiation Axis**: {Performance | Experience | Price | Niche | Innovation}

**Unique Advantages**:
1. {advantage} - {why it matters to customers}
2. {advantage} - {why it matters to customers}
3. {advantage} - {why it matters to customers}

**Competitive Comparison**:
| Factor | Us | Alternative A | Alternative B |
|--------|-----|---------------|---------------|
| {factor} | {our position} | {their position} | {their position} |

**Defensibility**:
- {What makes this sustainable}
- {Barriers to copying}
- {What strengthens over time}

### Value Score Breakdown

| Dimension | Score | Assessment |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| Clarity | {1-5} | {Is the value obvious?} |
| Relevance | {1-5} | {Does customer care?} |
| Differentiation | {1-5} | {Is it unique/better?} |
| Credibility | {1-5} | {Is it believable?} |
| Defensibility | {1-5} | {Can we sustain it?} |
| **TOTAL** | **{/25}** | |

### Positioning Recommendations

**Target Segments** (prioritized):
1. {segment name} - {why good fit} - {approach}
2. {segment name} - {why good fit} - {approach}

**Key Messages**:
- Primary: {main message}
- Supporting: {supporting message}
- Proof: {proof point needed}

**Channel Strategy**:
- {where to reach customers}
- {marketing approach}

**Pricing Guidance**:
- {pricing model recommendation}
- {price point rationale}
- {value-based pricing justification}
```
