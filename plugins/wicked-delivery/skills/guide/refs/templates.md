# Learning Guide Templates

Detailed templates for learning paths and task recommendations.

## First Task Suggestions Template

```markdown
## First Task Suggestions: {Project Name}

### Your Profile
**Experience**: {Assessed level - Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced}
**Goal**: {Inferred or stated objective}
**Estimated ramp-up**: {Timeline based on complexity}

### Quick Wins (Start Here)

These tasks build confidence and validate your environment setup:

#### 1. {Task name}
**Type**: {Documentation/Testing/Bug fix}
**Estimated time**: {1-4 hours}
**Why this task**: {Learning objective}

**What you'll learn**:
- {Skill/concept 1}
- {Skill/concept 2}

**How to start**:
```bash
# Concrete steps
{Command 1}
{Command 2}
```

**Success criteria**:
- [ ] {Checkpoint 1}
- [ ] {Checkpoint 2}

**Resources**:
- {File to read}
- {Test to run}

---

#### 2. {Next task}
{Same structure}

### After Quick Wins

Once you've completed 1-2 quick wins:
- {Next step}
- {Progressive challenge}

### Getting Unstuck

If you hit blockers:
1. {Resource 1}
2. {Resource 2}
3. Ask in {communication channel}
```
